Debrief — What this study was about

This survey is part of a PhD research study examining how Pakistani consumers respond to AI-generated advertising. The brand "Luminara Beauty" is a fictional brand created specifically for this research — it does not represent any real product.

The study explores whether the disclosure of AI-generated content affects consumer trust, perceived authenticity, and purchase intention. Your honest responses contribute directly to academic understanding of digital advertising in Pakistan.

If you have any questions about this research, please reach out to the researcher. Jazakallah Khair / Thank you!
